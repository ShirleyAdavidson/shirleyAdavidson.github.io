Services & Prices

Registration

By registering now with our cremation service, you will save your family or friends from having to make difficult decisions at an emotional time, when it would be a great burden to them.

As a registered member, you will have all the details taken care of by us at the time of need. Prepayment for the cost of service is not required. If you do wish to prepay at the time of registration, no additional fees will have to be paid at the time of death, as the funds will be held in your name, with our organization as a beneficiary.

Completion of the registration form in advance, authorizes us to carry out your wishes and act promptly as directed. Upon payment of the one-time registration fee, you will become a lifetime member and will receive a membership card.

The registration fee is $15.00 per person. The registration fee is non-refundable. This fee is waived if services are paid in full. Funds will be held in trust until the time of need.

Cremation Service - $795.00

We will take care of all details at the time of need, thus saving family members from having to make arrangements at that time.

Our services include:

  • Removal from place of death within San Diego County
  • Obtaining attending physician's signature for death certificate
  • Preparation and filing of disposition permit
  • Cremation
  • Preparation of death notice for newspaper, if desired by family
  • Completion and forwarding to Social Security Administration of form SSA 721 for reimbursement of death benefits to family, if applicable
  • Completion and forwarding to Veterans Administration of Form 21-530 for reimbursement of burial allowance to the family, if applicable
  • Procuring and forwarding the American flag to the family
  • Scheduling services at a State Veterans Cemetery
