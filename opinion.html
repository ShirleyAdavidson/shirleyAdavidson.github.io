Good 1

The world wildlife website for elephants allows readers to get a lot of information regarding these fabulous animals. The overall website is clean and easy to follow. The website has links to many others regarding people who are experts on elephants and wildlife conservation. Along with these links there are links to other pages regarding information about elephants. When comparing this website to the others I find it to be very inviting for the reader. Some little things that the site has that makes it stand out from the others is that there is a map showing where the two different types of elephants live. Along with current articles about different relevant events like the aftermath of China as it bans the ivory trade.

The navigation menu on the site underlines what part of the page you are on allowing the reader to know what they are looking at as the title on some sections is not in view. Each section of the page has a different background color making it easy for the reader to know when they have moved to the next section. The main colors that are used are black, white, and gray; with a little orange and blue text for things that are linked to a different page or website. There are widgets that are linked to facebook, instagram, and youtube. When looking at the code for this site it is easy to follow. The developers are using what looks like the current version of html, along with the use of script.
